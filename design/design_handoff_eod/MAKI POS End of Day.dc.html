End-of-Day & Closing — on the new language

The cash-drawer closing flow migrated to the elevated system — the editable End-of-Day form, its immutable closed view, and the Closing History list. Raw Material Card sections, filled TextFormFields, and ExpansionTile rows become soft-shadow AppCard surfaces with the app's field styling; Cupertino → Lucide. The variance color semantics are preserved exactly — balanced = green, short = red, over = amber — along with the success "closed" banner, the amber post-close warning + "After close" recalculation, and full dark parity (gold leads).
