Handoff 12 · Users · migration

Users — on the new language

The admin-only User Management surface migrated to the elevated system. The biggest fix is color: the hard-coded Material role swatches duplicated across the tile and the form — admin red, staff green, cashier orange — are lifted into theme-aware role/status tokens with *OnDark parity variants. Material Cards become soft-shadow AppCards, the role picker becomes selectable AppCard rows, Cupertino → Lucide. Neutral-by-default: color is reserved for the role badge, the You / Inactive tags, and the destructive action.

AppCard surfaces — Card → soft shadow, selectable role rows
Role tokens — Material swatches → theme-aware + dark parity
Lucide — replaces Cupertino
Neutral discipline — color only for role / status / destructive
