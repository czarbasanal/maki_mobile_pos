Job Orders — the service ticket, elevated

The old Drafts feature, repurposed into motorcycle service tickets: an open ticket carries parts, labor lines, an assigned mechanic and a motorcycle model, then bills out into a Sale (the ticket survives an abandoned checkout). The routed screens migrate to the elevated system — the list, its empty state, the New Job Order dialog, the editor (parts · labor · summary · bill-out), the add-parts sheet, the delete confirm and the admin reports. Raw Material item rows become soft-shadow AppCards; Cupertino glyphs → Lucide. Job orders have no status, so the neutral-by-default discipline holds — color is reserved for slate primary affordances (total · qty badge · service badge · Open / Bill out) and green for a discount; only delete carries red. The POS badge is re-iconed to a clipboard so it reads as “open job orders,” not a stuck cart count. Currency renders grouped (₱1,430.00), SKUs stay mono, full dark parity (gold leads).
