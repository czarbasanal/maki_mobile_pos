Void Requests — on the new language

The admin void-approval queue migrated to the elevated system. A raw Material ListView of ListTiles split by dividers becomes soft-shadow AppCard rows; Cupertino → Lucide; and the status that currently ships all-neutral gains color semantics — pending amber, approved green, rejected red — with dark parity. The unread red dot, "Mark all read", tap-to-resolve flow, and the password/reason dialogs are all preserved; the resolve sheet's receipt breakdown matches the shipped Sale Detail (bundle 03). Admin-only.
