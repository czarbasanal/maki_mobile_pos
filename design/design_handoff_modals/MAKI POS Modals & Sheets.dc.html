A separate channel

A transient bar at the bottom — 1.5px status border + lightened fill, dismissible. Stacked here for reference; in use it's one at a time. showSuccess / Warning / ErrorSnackBar stay their own channel — never a replacement for a confirm dialog.

Rules
Bottom-docked, above the SafeArea — never centered.
One at a time; newest replaces. Dismiss via × or swipe.
