Purchase Orders — reorder flow, elevated

The shipped-functional Reorder feature gets the elevated treatment. Every friction point from the handoff is addressed: flush cards become padded AppCards with glyph tiles; the triple-duty ChoiceChips split into a segmented window picker, a segmented view toggle and restyled filter chips; bare checkbox/stepper rows become card rows with stepper pills; the detail's ragged Wrap becomes a pinned footer with one primary per status; the FAB is retired for an app-bar +; dates go friendly (Jul 3, 9:41 AM); empty states get the 86px tile; the cap warning uses the amber-note pattern. POs have a real status enum, so the four-status color language stays — as tokens (draft neutral · ordered amber · received green · cancelled red), light + dark. Naming unified: screens say Purchase Orders (the dashboard pill keeps its action verb "Reorder"), and the save button says what it does — "Create 3 purchase orders." Costs stay off the cards deliberately (the CSV to the supplier is cost-free). SKUs and PO refs render Roboto Mono; Lucide 1.75 throughout; full dark parity (gold leads).
