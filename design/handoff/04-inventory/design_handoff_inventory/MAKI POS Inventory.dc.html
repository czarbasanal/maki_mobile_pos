Inventory — on the new language

All three Inventory screens migrated to the elevated system. Mixed elevation is fixed — summary stats and product rows now share one soft-shadow AppCard surface (no more Material-default shadows next to flat hairline boxes). Cupertino icons → Lucide. The long product form is grouped into sectioned cards with a pinned submit; price-history rows are wrapped in a card. Slate primary, gold in dark.
