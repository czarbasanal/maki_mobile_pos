Handoff 10a · Settings hub · migration

Settings — hub, theme & about

Slice 10a of the largest bundle — the Settings hub (admin + role-gated non-admin), the theme picker sheet, and the standalone About screen. Raw Material Card+ListTile+Divider groups become soft-shadow grouped AppCards with inset hairlines; Cupertino glyphs → Lucide; the native Switch / RadioListTile get themed treatments. The neutral-by-default discipline holds — the only color is the slate/gold primary (chevrons stay muted) and the role pill, which carries genuine role semantics: admin = red #F44336 (retained — the shipped color), staff = blue, cashier = green. The Administration section is admin-only (frame 2 shows it gone). Full dark parity (gold leads).
