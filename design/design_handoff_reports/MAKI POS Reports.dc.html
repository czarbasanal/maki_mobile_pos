Reports — on the new language

All four Reports surfaces migrated to the elevated system. Cupertino icons → Lucide; flat Material Card surfaces → soft-shadow AppCard. The color discipline is preserved exactly — neutral by default, color only for status (green profit, red voids) and the amber/silver/bronze rank medals — with full dark parity (gold leads). Role gating stays intact: daily-reports roles get a "today only" banner instead of the date picker, and Total Cost / Gross Profit / Service / per-row profit badges remain admin-only.
